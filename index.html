About Me

Ashtyn Nieves

I'm a self taught frontend software engineer with a great talent for creating responsive websites. Although I do enjoy making and sharing great user experiences my true passion is in the creation and coding of video games. When I was younger I didn't have much time to really think about what I wanted as an individual. Learning to code has saved me in more ways than one and its helped me finally understand who I can be.

The Journey

In middle school I was too focused on being the perfect student and the perfect daughter. I joined drama club right at the beginning of 6th grade as stage crew and between moving sets, fixing lights and costumes my only goal was to be the perfect member of our club. Thats who I was until I graduated and become a highschool student. Even in highschool I left no room for thought just progress. I was a student, athlete, and part-time employee all at the age of 14. When I turned 16 my junior year in highschool I joined CTEC which was a career technical program and I was in the Law Enforcement Program. Everyday from 6 in the morning till 10 at night my time wasn't my own but I enjoyed what I did. A perfect student doesn't have the ability to be anything more than a perfect student. Life and decisions both in and out of my hands brought me to a place where after I graduated I was lost.
